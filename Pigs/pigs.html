Pigs are omnivourous and large mammals and can handle quite alot of different food and smart brains they have smalll bristly fur, sharp teeth, and short curly tails sometimes. Scientists have done labortorary tests on pigs such as mazes, and simple puzzles and according to the results pigs are fairly smart.

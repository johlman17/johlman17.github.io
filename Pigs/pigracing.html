Pig racing, nobody would really think that this is a thing but, it is. Its on a specific Youtube channel called League of Pigs. It consists of 5 pigs racing and several different seasons (the first of which below). There are 5 different pigs: Ginger Hamilton, Piggy Smalls, Hoshi Oinku, Pepper Sanchez, and Bear Trotsky. Its a very competive competition even having a narrator and replays. Its a very cool channel and I recommend this to anyone who loves sports, animals, or both!
